INTRODUCTION 项目简介
The frequent phenomenon of staying up late has seriously affected people's life. Our project wants to design a product to help people reduce staying up late.

Product Concept Map

产品概念图

Internal resolution Vision
PreviousNext

At night, the user lies in bed, and the mobile phone is automatically connected with the Intelligent pillow. He can receive messages by listening and sending messages by speaking. When he receives messages, he does not have to look at the mobile phone.Users can also choose to let their phones transmit music, stories or news to the headset through voice.If the device in the pillow detects that the user has entered the sleep state, the mobile phone will block the information brought by the outside world, such as social software, to avoid disturbing the user, while the original alarm clock and other functions of the mobile phone are still effective.

Hallucination
It is an illusion that people will not fall asleep after lying in bed, but spend their time playing with mobile phones and other recreational activities. When using the "Intelligent pillow", users can know their sleep status. It can give users psychological hints and reduce the use of mobile phones. The "Intelligent pillow" guides users to make sleep choices, creating the illusion that users can go to bed early by ther own. Users will also continue to improve their work and rest in the process of using.
